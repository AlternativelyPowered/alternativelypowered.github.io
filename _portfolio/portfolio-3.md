Completed as part of a group project, this presentation focuses on resources that benefit individuals searching for historical information about deceased individuals. Detailing a wide range of sources from scholarly to free-to-use, this presentation highlights many of the standard reference materials that L.I.S. professionals use in their reference work. Completed Fall 2025.
